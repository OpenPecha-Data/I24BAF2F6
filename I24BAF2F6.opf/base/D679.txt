A short history of Shakyamuni
of the Main Temple of Lhasa
when the Buddha Shakyamuni thought to pass
into Nirvana, the god of wisdom Manjushri prayed
for figure out one question that if the Buddha went
into Nirvana who will replace you as Buddha and to
prostrate and to ask for blessing by faithfuls. The
Buddha Shakymuni smiled and kindled four different
lights to different places, those lights reached with
four gods which are the Tshanspa and the Khybjug
and the Brgyabyin and the heaven setylist Shakama,
Those three main gods gave salute to the Buddha and
made promise to build three images of Buddha, the
image of the Dhama and the image of the prophecy
and the the image of incarnation as been it's
benefactors. The heaven craftsman Shakama also



gave a salute to the Buddha and promised to make
the designer of the three imges. The Buddha also let.
those gods to finish their promises. The Tsanspa
build an image of the Buddha which formed by
varied treasures and placed in the heaven. The
khybjug build an image of the Buddha Wshich
formed by varied treasures and pleced in the ocean.
The Brgybyin also build three images of the
Buddha which as aged eight and aged twelve and
aged twenty-five.
And consecrated by the Buddha himself, and
told to this three gods that the images have the same
ability like the Buddha himself. The image of the
Buddha which aged twenty – five as offerings and
asked tor blessing by the god himself and the image
of the Buddha which aged twelve brought into
Afghanitan by The Dakinis, after five hundreds
years late this image again brought into underworld.
ofter other many years late the image of the Buddha
brought into Maghada by a Yogis named Shilenta
and an Abbot named skrargyal.
Once a principality of India have been invaded
by the army of no - Buddhism and the Buddhism
was destroyed by this army.
The king of this kingdom made friend with
Chinese emperor and got support from the Chinese
emperor and also got ultimate truimph. At that time
the Chinese emperor asked for the image of Buddha
which aged twelve to his kingdom as offerings, at the
same time the emperor of Chinese also asked for four
kind of Sutras and four abbots from the principality
of India. The king of the principality of India present
those precious to China.
At the time of Qin dynasty brought the image
of the Buddha into Changan as an oferings.
At the time of Tibetan king Songtsan Gampo,
this king send his clavest minister Mgartontsan to
Nepal for ask the Gongjo of Nepal king Debana as
his queen, at the same time brought the image of
Buddha which aged eight into Tibet. late the Tibetan



king Songtsan Gampo again send his clavest minister
Mgarstongtsan to China for ask the Gongjo the Tang
dynasty of Chinese as his other queen, and also by
this time brought the image of Buddha which aged
twelve into Tibet.
When the tow Gongjo reached Tibet each of the
Gongjo build a temple for their special offerings.
When the king of Tibetan Songtsan Gampo
died, the Chinese army came to Tibet and want to
robbed the image of Buddha back to China, But the
Chinese army did not found where the image has
been, because the queen of Chinese hidden this
image at back of an image of Manjushri the god of
windom. When the second Gongjo of the Tang
dynasty of Chinese came to Tibet as Tibetan king's
queen, she took out the image of the Buddha, and as
a precious offerings oblated at the main temple of
Lhasa.
At the early time of the Tibetan king Trisong
Detsan the Tibet has been controlled by his two main
ministers those named Khrompa Skyes and Stakskra
klekon, thesi two ministers dislike Buddhism so they
want send the image of the Buddha back to China,
but for many supernatural reasons they did not done
that, so they hidden the image of the Buddha under
of sand beach.
When the Tibetan king Trisong Detsan began
to control the Tibet by himself, he drived the two
ministers out of Tibet and brought the image of
Buddha back to the main temple of Lhasa.
When the time of the Tibetan king Wudum
Tsanpo began to destroy the Buddhism and buddhist
temples, and change the Buddhists to other religious
believers by face, his some ministers hidden the
image of the Buddha under sand beach. After the
Tibetan king Wudum Tsanpo has been killed by a
Buddhist monk named Lhalun dbalrdor, his two sons
and the two king of the two principalities of Tibet
out the image of the Buddha and as their invaluable
offerings.

When the greatest master of the Tibet
Zhonkhapa became the abbot of Dkaldan monastery,
he let his follows to decorated the image of the

festival.
When the time of fifth Dalai lama and his
minister Sangrgs Rgymtsho, the two great rebuild
and enlarged the two temples of Lhasa in the two
temples the image of the Buddhas has been oblated
